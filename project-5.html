The Energy Cycle

Computer-Aided Design (CAD) SOLIDWORKS Machine Design

Overview

This project is a compact under the desk exercise bike that converts the kinetic energy from pedaling into electrical power to charge USB devices, making it perfect for office workers who want to stay active while working. It's designed to be portable and foldable, encouraging people to reduce their electricity consumption and improve their health at the same time.
